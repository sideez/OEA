Operation Earth Action

Our National Service Project encouraging all GGC Members to work together and make a difference in the world! Operation Earth Action focuses on action and learning by doing service for the environment in fun and inspiring ways.

Operation Earth Action has six fun and exciting activities to help girls and Units make a personal impact on the environment and take action for a better world:

  1. Pledge to use resuable: water bottles and/or shopping bags
  2. Map your Meal
  3. Homemade cleaning and personal care products
  4. Plant a Tree
  5. Be an energy detective

Who

This project is open to all Girl Guides of Canada Members as part of their Unit activities or individual participation. Non-members can also show their support by participating with a Unit activity (e.g. at a tree planting event) or with an individual Member (i.e. friends pledging with a Guiding Member to use reusable shopping bags).

Recognition

Earn your Operation Earth Action crest by doing at least two activities.
